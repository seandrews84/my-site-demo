Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I miss a class?

If you miss a class, check the course website for lecture notes and any assignments. Contact a classmate for additional information, and then visit during office hours if you have specific questions about the material.

Is extra help available outside of regular office hours?

Yes! While our regular office hours are listed above, we understand that schedules can be tight. Contact Professor Andrews via email to arrange additional help sessions or virtual meetings as needed.

How can I prepare for the final project?

Start thinking about project ideas early in the semester. We recommend reviewing past projects and beginning to form project teams at least a month before the final project period begins. Regular consultations with the teaching staff during the planning phase are strongly encouraged.
